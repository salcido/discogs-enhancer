BACKUP / RESTORE UTILITY

Use this interface to backup your current configuration or migrate your configuration between browsers. Please use caution when backing up and restoring your data. Malformed data can break the extenison.

Backup Preferences

Click the Copy Preferences button to copy your current preference configuration to your clipboard. If you are planning on saving this data in a text document, please make sure it is Plain Text.

Restore Preferences

Paste your saved preferences into the textbox below and click Save Preferences.

If you have issues with restoring, click here to post in the Discogs Enhancer group and I'll help you sort things out.

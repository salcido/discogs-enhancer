Follow these 3 steps to add Discogs Enhancer to your brower's Toolbar:

Step One:

Click the puzzle piece icon. This will open a list of all the extensions currently installed in your browser. Look through it to find Discogs Enhancer.

Step Two:

Once you've found Discogs Enhancer on the list - click the pin icon to keep Discogs Enhancer in the browser's toolbar.

Step Three:

Click the record icon to show the Discogs Enhancer menu. From here you can enable and disable features. Hover over the question mark icons (?) to see a brief explanation of each feature. Click LEARN for in-depth instructions.
